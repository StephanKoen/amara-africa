## How the body formatting works

The `Body` is written in light markdown. The renderer understands:

- `## Heading` → a major **section heading** (one per stop, e.g. `## Serengeti — 4 nights`).
  These also anchor the per-section photo strip, so use one `##` per stop.
- `### Subheading` → a smaller italic sub-heading inside a section.
- `**Stay: Lodge Name**` on its own line → a small gold italic label.
- `---` on its own line → a thin divider between sections.
- Blank line between paragraphs.

Each journey already has **3 photos** — tell me which stop each should sit
under (or leave it and I'll distribute them evenly across the sections).
